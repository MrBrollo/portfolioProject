About Me

So how did i get into programming?

It all started when I was a kid, I simply loved play videogames, especially the ones centered around puzzles, strategies and exploration. I've always been a curious kid, and growing older I started asking myself why are things made the way they are? I used to spend a lot of hours sitting in front of my PC just playing or browsing the Internet, as I saw it as an opportunity to learn even more. Studying HTML, CSS and programming in general is an experience that makes for personal growth and the more I learn, the more I love it!
